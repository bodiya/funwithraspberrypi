1. Create a ThingSpeak account: https://thingspeak.com/users/sign_up

2. Go to Channels: https://thingspeak.com/channels

3. Create a new channel by clicking the "Create New Channel" button.

4. Fill in the following fields:
 - Name: (pick one, or leave it)
 - Description: Internet of Things exercise from the Fun With Raspberry Pi course.
 - Field 1: degrees F

5. Go the the "API Keys" tab

6. Copy down your "Write API Key".  You'll use this in the exercise.

BE AWARE: ThingSpeak has a rate limit of 4 updates per minute (one every 15 seconds)
